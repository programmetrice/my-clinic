Appointments at My Clinic

My Clinic works with hundreds of insurance companies and is an in-network provider for millions of people. In most cases, My Clinic doesn't require a physician referral. Some insurers require referrals, or may have additional requirements for certain medical care. All appointments are prioritized on the basis of medical need, and the team members who will care for you or your family have the expertise and skills to provide the best care possible.
